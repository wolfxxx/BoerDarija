A2 Module 15: Giving Opinions & Making Suggestions

Learn how to express your thoughts and ideas, and suggest activities to others in Darija.

Expressing Your Opinion

Here are common ways to share what you think.

English Phrase Darija (Script) Transcription Example / Notes Audio
In my opinion... ف رأيي... F rray-i... F rray-i, had l-film zwin. (In my opinion, this film is nice.)
I think that... كانضن بلي... / كايبان ليا بلي... KanDenn belli... / Kayban li-ya belli... KanDenn belli ghadi ykun l-berd ghdda. (I think it will be cold tomorrow.)
For me... / According to me... بالنسبة ليا... B n-nisba li-ya... B n-nisba li-ya, atay 7sen mn l-qhwa. (For me, tea is better than coffee.)
It seems to me that... كايبان ليا بلي... Kayban li-ya belli... Kayban li-ya belli huwa mashi hna. (It seems to me he is not here.)
I agree. متافق(ة). Mettafeq (m) / Mettafqa (f). Ana mettafeq m3ak. (I agree with you.)
I don't agree. ما متافقش / ما متافقاش. Ma mettafeq-sh (m) / Ma mettafqa-sh (f).
`belli` (بلي): This word often means "that" when introducing a clause after verbs of thinking, saying, or believing.

Making Suggestions

How to propose an idea or activity.

English Phrase Darija (Script) Transcription Example / Notes Audio
Let's go... يلاه نمشيو... Yallah nmshiw... Yallah nmshiw l s-sinima. (Let's go to the cinema.)
What do you think if we...? / How about we...? أشنو بان ليك إلا / إلى...? Ashnu ban lik ila...? Ashnu ban lik ila nmshiw ntghddaw برا (outside)? (What do you think if we go eat lunch out?)
Why don't we...? علاش ما نمشيوش / نديروش...? 3lash ma nmshiwsh / ndirush...? 3lash ma nshrbush atay daba? (Why don't we drink tea now?)
Maybe we can... يمكن لينا... / نقدروا... Yemken lina... / Nqdru... Yemken lina nsafr ghdda. (Maybe we can travel tomorrow.)
Good idea! فكرة مزيانة! Fikra mzyana!
I don't want to / I don't feel like it. ما بغيتش. / ما فياش. Ma bghitsh. / Ma fiya-sh. `Ma fiya-sh` means "It's not in me (to do it)."
`Yallah` (يلاه): A very common and versatile word meaning "Let's go," "Come on," or used to initiate an action.

Dialogue: Planning an Outing

Sara: السلام أحمد، لاباس؟
Ssalām Ahmed, labas?
Hello Ahmed, how are you?

Ahmed: و عليكم السلام سارة، لاباس الحمد لله. و نتي؟
Wa 3likum ssalam Sara, labas l-7amdullah. U nti?
Hello Sara, fine thanks to God. And you?

Sara: حتى أنا لاباس. أشنو بان ليك إلا نمشيو للقهوة هاد العشية؟
7tta ana labas. Ashnu ban lik ila nmshiw l l-qhwa had l-3shiya?
I'm fine too. What do you think if we go to the café this evening?

Ahmed: فكرة مزيانة! ف رأيي، القهوة اللي حدا المحطة زوينة.
Fikra mzyana! F rray-i, l-qhwa lli 7da l-m7atta zwina.
Good idea! In my opinion, the café next to the station is nice.

Sara: متافقة. يلاه نمشيو ف الستة؟
Mettafqa. Yallah nmshiw f s-setta?
I agree. Let's go at six?

Ahmed: واخا، مزيان. نتلاقاو تما.
Wakha, mzyan. Ntlaqaw temma.
Okay, good. We'll meet there. (`Ntlaqaw` = We meet/will meet)

Practice Time!

Exercise 1: Your Opinion

Complete the sentences with your opinion (use an adjective).

  1. F rray-i, Darija sahla / s3iba. (easy/difficult)
  2. B n-nisba li-ya, l-makla l-mghribia (Moroccan food) bnina. (delicious)
  3. KanDenn belli had l-ktab mumti3. (interesting)

Exercise 2: Making Suggestions

Translate these suggestions into Darija:

  1. Let's eat tagine today. Yallah naklu ttajin l-yum.
  2. What do you (f.s.) think if we watch a film? Ashnu ban lik ila ntferrju f shi film?
  3. Why don't we travel next week? 3lash ma nsafRush s-simana j-jaya?
  4. Maybe we can go to the market. Yemken lina nmshiw l s-souq.

Exercise 3: Agree or Disagree

Your friend says: "هاد المطعم غالي بزاف!" (Had l-mat3am ghali bzzaf! - This restaurant is very expensive!). How do you respond if you agree? How if you disagree?

Agree: Mettafeq(a). / Bssa7, ghali. (True, it's expensive)

Disagree: La, ma mettafeq-sh. Mashi ghali. (No, I don't agree. It's not expensive.)

Being able to share your thoughts and make plans with others is a big step in language learning! Try suggesting something to a friend (even if it's just practice).

Module 16: Talking about Locations & Basic Directions

Learn vocabulary for common places and how to ask for and give simple directions in Darija.

Asking for Locations

The most common way to ask "Where is...?" uses the question word `Fin` (Where?). You can combine this with `kayen` (masculine) or `kayna` (feminine) for "Where is there...?" or often just use `Fin` + [The Place].

English Question Darija (Script) Transcription Notes Audio
Where is the market? فين السوق؟ / فين كاين السوق؟ Fin s-souq? / Fin kayen s-souq? Souq is Masc.
Where is the pharmacy? فين الفارماسيان؟ / فين كاين الفارماسيان؟ Fin l-farmasiyan? / Fin kayen l-farmasiyan? Farmasiyan is Masc.
Where is the station? فين المحطة؟ / فين كاينة المحطة؟ Fin l-m7atta? / Fin kayna l-m7atta? M7atta is Fem.
How do I go to the bank? كيفاش نمشي للبانكة؟ Kifash nmshi l l-banka? Kifash = How, nmshi = I go, l = to
Is it near? واش قريب؟ Wash qrib? Qrib (M) / Qriba (F)
Is it far? واش بعيد؟ Wash b3id? B3id (M) / B3ida (F)
Remember to use `3afak` (please) when asking strangers for directions: `3afak, fin s-souq?`

Here are the essential words and phrases for giving simple directions.

Direction Phrases:

English Phrase Darija (Script) Transcription Audio
Go straight ahead.سير نيشان.Sir nishan.
Turn right.دور على اليمين.Dur 3la limen.
Turn left.دور على اليسر.Dur 3la lisr.
It's on the right.كاين(ة) على اليمين.Kayen(a) 3la limen.
It's on the left.كاين(ة) على اليسر.Kayen(a) 3la lisr.
It's near here.قريب(ة) من هنا.Qrib(a) mn hna.
It's far from here.بعيد(ة) من هنا.B3id(a) mn hna.
It's next to the...حدا ال...7da l-...
Verb Forms: `Sir` is the command "Go!" (to m.s.). `Dur` is the command "Turn!" (to m.s.). Remember these change for f.s. (`siri`, `duri`) and plural (`siru`, `duru`). Use `Kayen` or `Kayna` depending on the gender of the place you're describing. `Qrib/B3id` also change for gender (`qriba/b3ida`).

Dialogue: Asking for Directions

Person A: السلام عليكم. عافاك، فين كاينة البانكة؟
Ssalām 3likum. 3afak, fin kayna l-banka?
Hello. Please, where is the bank?

Person B: و عليكم السلام. سير نيشان، و من بعد دور على ليسر. البانكة كاينة حدا الفارماسيان.
Wa 3likum ssalam. Sir nishan, u mn ba3d dur 3la lisr. L-banka kayna 7da l-farmasiyan.
Hello. Go straight ahead, and after turn left. The bank is next to the pharmacy.

Person A: واش بعيدة من هنا؟
Wash b3ida mn hna?
Is it far from here?

Person B: لا، ماشي بعيدة بزاف. قريبة.
La, mashi b3ida bzzaf. Qriba.
No, not very far. It's near.

Person A: شكراً بزاف!
Shukran bzzaf!
Thank you very much!

Person B: العفو.
L-3fu.
You're welcome.

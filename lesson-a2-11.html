Asking for and Buying Tickets

Here are some phrases you might use at a ticket counter.

English Phrase Darija (Script) Transcription Audio
I want a ticket to Rabat, please. بغيت واحد الورقة ل الرباط، عافاك. Bghit wa7ed l-werqa l Rbat, 3afak.
How much is a ticket to Marrakech? بشحال الورقة ل مراكش؟ Bsh7al l-werqa l Mrraksh?
When does the train/bus leave? إيمتى غادي يخرج التران/الطوبيس؟ Imta ghadi ykhruj t-tran/t-tobis?
When does it arrive? إيمتى غادي يوصل؟ Imta ghadi yiwsel?
One way or round trip? ألي ولا ألي روتور؟ Allez wella allez-retour?
One way, please. غير ألي، عافاك. Ghir allez, 3afak.
Is this the bus to Fes? واش هادا هو الطوبيس ديال فاس؟ Wash hada huwa t-tobis dyal Fas?
`safr` (to travel):
  • Past: Ana `safert` (I traveled), Huwa `safer`, Hiya `safret`.
  • Present: Ana `kansafr`, Huwa `kaysafr`.
  • Future: Ana `ghadi nsafr`.

Describing a Simple Journey (Past Tense)

Person A: فين مشيتي السيمانة اللي فاتت؟
Fin mshiti s-simana lli fatet?
Where did you go last week?

Person B: مشيت ل مراكش.
Mshit l Mrraksh.
I went to Marrakech.

Person A: بزاف! كيفاش مشيتي؟ بالتران؟
Bzzaf! Kifash mshiti? B t-tran?
Great! How did you go? By train?

Person B: إييه، سافرت بالتران. كان زوين. و من بعد، خديت طاكسي صغير ل لوطيل.
Iyyeh, safert b t-tran. Kan zwin. U mn ba3d, khdit taxi sghir l l'otel.
Yes, I traveled by train. It was nice. And after that, I took a petit taxi to the hotel.

Person A: مزيان. شحال بقيتي تما؟
Mzyan. Sh7al bqiti temma?
Good. How long did you stay there?

Person B: بقيت تلاتة ديال ليام.
Bqit tlata dyal l-iyam.
I stayed three days.

Dialogue Vocab:
  • `khdit` (خديت): I took (from `khda` - to take)
  • `bqit` (بقيت): I stayed (from `bqa` - to stay)
  • `iyam` (أيام): days (plural of `yum` - day)

Exercise 2: At the Station

Translate these phrases to Darija:

  1. I want a ticket to Casablanca, please. Bghit werqa l Kaza, 3afak.
  2. When does the train leave? Imta ghadi ykhruj t-tran?

Exercise 3: Your Last Trip

Write 2-3 sentences in Darija about a trip you took (real or imaginary). Mention where you went, how you traveled, and maybe how long you stayed. Use past tense.

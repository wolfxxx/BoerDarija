Basic Bargaining Phrases

Bargaining is common in traditional markets (souks) but usually not in modern stores with fixed prices. Keep it polite and friendly!

English Darija Script Transcription Audio
Expensive!غالي!Ghali!
Very expensive!غالي بزاف!Ghali bzzaf!
Not expensive.ماشي غالي.Mashi ghali.
Lower the price a little, please.نقص شوية، عافاك.Neqqs shwiya, 3afak.
What's the last price?أخير تمن؟Akhir taman?
Make a good price (for me).دير شي تمن مزيان.Dir shi taman mzyan.
Okay, I'll take it.صافي، بغيتو/بغيتها.Safi, bghitu/bghitha.(bghitu for masc. item, bghitha for fem.)
No, thank you. (Leave it).لا، شكراً. / خليه.La, shukran. / Khllih.(Khllih = Leave it - masc.)
Bargaining Etiquette: Smile, be polite, don't offer an insultingly low price. Decide beforehand what you're willing to pay. Sometimes the first price is very high, expecting you to bargain. If you agree on a price, you should generally buy the item.

Shopping Dialogue Example (in a Souk)

Let's see how these phrases might be used when buying a small tagine pot (masculine noun: طاجين - tajin).

Customer: السلام عليكم. واش عندك طاجين صغير؟
Ssalām 3likum. Wash 3ndek tajin sghir?
Hello. Do you have a small tagine pot?

Seller: و عليكم السلام. إييه، عندي هادا.
Wa 3likum ssalam. Iyyeh, 3ndi hada.
Hello. Yes, I have this one.

Customer: زوين. بشحال هادا؟
Zwin. Bsh7al hada?
It's nice. How much is this?

Seller: هادا ب سبعين درهم.
Hada b sb3in derhem.
This one is 70 dirhams.

Customer: أووو، غالي بزاف! نقص شوية، عافاك.
Ooooh, ghali bzzaf! Neqqs shwiya, 3afak.
Ooh, very expensive! Lower it a little, please.

Seller: واخا. ستين درهم، أخير تمن.
Wakha. Settin derhem, akhir taman.
Okay. 60 dirhams, final price.

Customer: صافي، مزيان. بغيتو.
Safi, mzyan. Bghitu.
Okay, good. I want it.

Exercise 1: Match the Phrase

Match the English phrase with the Darija transcription.

  1. How much is this (f.)?
  2. Lower the price, please.
  3. Very expensive!
  4. Do you have...?
  5. Okay, I want it (m.).
  • A. Ghali bzzaf!
  • B. Safi, bghitu.
  • C. Wash 3ndek...?
  • D. Bsh7al hadi?
  • E. Neqqs shwiya, 3afak.

Answers: 1-D, 2-E, 3-A, 4-C, 5-B

Exercise 2: Role Play Prompts

How would you say the following in Darija?

  1. Ask a seller: "Do you have mint tea (atay b n-n3na3)?" Wash 3ndek atay b n-n3na3?
  2. Ask the price: "How much is this (m.)?" Bsh7al hada?
  3. React to a price: "Expensive!" Ghali!
  4. Ask for a better price: "Make a good price." Dir shi taman mzyan.
  5. Agree to buy (a feminine item): "Okay, I want it." Safi, bghitha.
Going to a local market (if possible) or even just practicing these dialogues out loud is the best way to get comfortable with shopping interactions!

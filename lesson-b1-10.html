B1 Module 10: More Connectors & Discourse Markers

Enhance your fluency and create more complex, coherent sentences by mastering a wider range of connecting words and phrases.

Review: Basic Conjunctions (A1/A2)

You've already learned some fundamental connectors:

  • `u` / `w` (و) - and
  • `wella` / `aw` (ولا / أو) - or
  • `walakin` (ولكن) - but
  • `7it` (حيت) - because
  • `ila` (إلى) - if

Now, let's explore more sophisticated ways to link your ideas.

Connectors for Contrast

Connector (Darija/Trans.) Meaning Example Sentence (Darija) Transcription Audio
وخا / wakha although, even though, okay وخا كان البرد، خرجنا. Wakha kan l-berd, khrejna. (Although it was cold, we went out.)
بالعكس / b l-3eks on the contrary, actually هو ماشي معصب، بالعكس، ضريف بزاف. Huwa mashi m3esseb, b l-3eks, drif bzzaf. (He's not angry, on the contrary, he's very kind.)
و لكن / walakin but, however (Review) بغيت نسافر ولكن ما عنديش لفلوس. Bghit nsafr walakin ma 3ndish l-flus. (I want to travel but I don't have money.)
مع ذلك / ma3a dalik despite that, nevertheless (more formal) كان مريض، و مع ذلك جا للخدمة. Kan mrid, u ma3a dalik ja l l-khedma. (He was sick, and despite that he came to work.)

Connectors for Cause & Reason

Connector (Darija/Trans.) Meaning Example Sentence (Darija) Transcription Audio
حيت / حيتاش / 7it / 7itash because (Review) ما جيتش حيت كنت مريض. Ma jitsh 7it kunt mrid. (I didn't come because I was sick.)
على ود / على قبل / 3la wedd / 3la qbel because of, for the sake of درت هادشي على ودك. Dert hadshi 3la weddek. (I did this because of you / for your sake.)
بسباب / bsbab because of (often for negative reasons) تعطلنا بسباب الطررافيك. T3ettelna bsbab t-trafik. (We were late because of the traffic.)

Connectors for Result & Consequence

Connector (Darija/Trans.) Meaning Example Sentence (Darija) Transcription Audio
داكشي علاش / dakshi 3lash that's why, so كان البرد، داكشي علاش لبست جاكيطة. Kan l-berd, dakshi 3lash lbest jakita. (It was cold, that's why I wore a jacket.)
إذن / إدا / idan / ida (less common in casual Darija) so, therefore (more formal) هو ما قراش، إذن ما نجحش. Huwa ma qrash, idan ma nje7sh. (He didn't study, therefore he didn't pass.)
و هكذا / u hakda and so, thus خدم مزيان و هكذا خدا ترقية. Khdem mzyan u hakda khda terqiya. (He worked well and so he got a promotion.) (`terqiya`=promotion)

Connectors for Adding Information

Connector (Darija/Trans.) Meaning Example Sentence (Darija) Transcription Audio
و / عاود / تاني / u / 3awd / tani and, also, again شريت الخضرة و عاود الفاكية. Shrit l-khdra u 3awd l-fakiya. (I bought vegetables and also fruit.)
بالإضافة إلى / b l-iDaafa ila in addition to (more formal) كايعرف الدارجة، بالإضافة إلى الفرنسوية. Kay3ref d-darija, b l-iDaafa ila l-fransawiya. (He knows Darija, in addition to French.)
حتى / 7tta also, even, too حتى أنا بغيت نمشي. 7tta ana bghit nmshi. (I also want to go. / Me too, I want to go.)
`3awd` and `tani` often imply "also" or "again" when adding a similar point or repeating an action. `Tani` can mean "second" or "again."

Practice Time!

Exercise 1: Choose the Best Connector

Fill in the blank with an appropriate connector from the lesson (e.g., wakha, 7it, dakshi 3lash, u 3awd, b l-3eks).

  1. Kunt 3iyyan, walakin / wakha mshit l l-khedma. (I was tired, BUT/ALTHOUGH I went to work.)
  2. Kay3jbni l-film 7it fih قصة (qissa - story) zwina. (I like the film BECAUSE it has a nice story.)
  3. Ma 3ndush l-flus, dakshi 3lash ma ghadi-sh ysafr. (He doesn't have money, THAT'S WHY he won't travel.)
  4. Kanbghi nqra l-ktub u 3awd ntferrej f l-aflam. (I like to read books AND ALSO watch films.)
  5. Ma bghitsh hada, b l-3eks, bghit لاخر (l-akher - the other one). (I don't want this one, ON THE CONTRARY, I want the other one.)

Exercise 2: Combine Sentences

Combine the two ideas into one sentence using an appropriate connector.

  1. It was raining. We stayed at home. (Use "because" or "that's why")
    Answer: Bqina f d-dar 7it kant sh-shta katصب (katSubb - falling). / Kant sh-shta katSubb, dakshi 3lash bqina f d-dar.
  2. She is smart. She is also kind. (Use "and also")
    Answer: Hiya dkiya u 3awd Drifa.
  3. I want to go out. I have a lot of work. (Use "but" or "although")
    Answer: Bghit nkhruj walakin 3ndi bzzaf d l-khedma. / Wakha 3ndi bzzaf d l-khedma, bghit nkhruj.
Using a variety of connectors will make your Darija sound much more fluent and natural, allowing you to express more complex relationships between your ideas.

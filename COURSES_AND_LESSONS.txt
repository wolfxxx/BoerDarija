BOERDARIJA - COURSES AND LESSONS
================================

A1 BEGINNER COURSE (19 Lessons)
-------------------------------

Module 0: Darija Sounds & Transcription
  File: lesson0.html
  Topic: Master the unique sounds of Darija and learn how to read the transcription system used in this course.

Module 1: Greetings, Politeness & Introductions
  File: lesson1.html
  Topic: Learn basic greetings, introductions, essential polite phrases, and subject pronouns.

Module 2: Verb 'To Be', Nouns & Gender
  File: lesson2.html
  Topic: Understand the 'zero copula' for is/am/are, learn about noun gender, and the definite article 'l-'.

Module 3: 'To Have' & Possession
  File: lesson3.html
  Topic: Learn to say "I have" using `3ndi` and express ownership with possessive suffixes and `dyal`.

Module 4: Numbers (1-100) & Telling Age
  File: lesson4.html
  Topic: Master counting from 1 to 100 and learn how to ask and state your age.

Module 5: Demonstratives & Common Objects
  File: lesson5.html
  Topic: Learn to use "this," "that," "these," and "those," and identify common objects.

Module 6: Adjectives & Agreement, Colors
  File: lesson6.html
  Topic: Learn how to describe nouns using adjectives, ensure agreement, and basic color vocabulary.

Module 7: Verb 'To Want', Food & Drink
  File: lesson7.html
  Topic: Master the verb "to want" (`bghit`) and learn vocabulary for common food and drinks.

Module 8: Present Tense (`ka-` verbs)
  File: lesson8.html
  Topic: Learn to talk about habits and regular actions using the `ka-` present tense marker.

Module 9: Asking Basic Questions
  File: lesson9.html
  Topic: Learn to ask Yes/No questions with `Wash` and use basic question words (Who, What, Where, When, How Much?).

Module 10: Days, Months & Time
  File: lesson10.html
  Topic: Learn the days of the week, months of the year, and how to tell time in Darija.

Module 11: Family Members
  File: lesson11.html
  Topic: Learn vocabulary for immediate family members and how to talk about your family.

Module 12: Prepositions of Place & Conjunctions
  File: lesson12.html
  Topic: Learn essential prepositions to describe location (in, on, under, next to...) and basic conjunctions (and, or).

Module 13: Negation
  File: lesson13.html
  Topic: Master negation using `ma-...-sh` for verbs, `mashi` for nouns/adjectives, and `ma 3ndish`.

Module 14: Imperatives (Commands)
  File: lesson14.html
  Topic: Learn how to give commands, both telling someone to do something and not to do something.

Module 15: Shopping & Bargaining
  File: lesson15.html
  Topic: Apply your A1 skills in practical shopping scenarios, including essential bargaining phrases.

Module 16: Locations & Directions
  File: lesson16.html
  Topic: Learn vocabulary for places and how to ask for and give basic directions.

Module 17: Expressing Likes/Dislikes
  File: lesson17.html
  Topic: Learn the common structure `kay3jbni...` to talk about what you like and dislike.

Module 18: A1 Review & Consolidation
  File: lesson18.html
  Topic: Review all the A1 topics, practice with mixed exercises, and prepare for the next level!


A2 ELEMENTARY COURSE (18 Lessons)
----------------------------------

Module 1: A1 Review & Intro to Past Tense
  File: lesson-a2-1.html
  Topic: Refresh A1 basics and learn the past tense for regular verbs, including past time expressions.

Module 2: Past Tense (Irregular Verbs)
  File: lesson-a2-2.html
  Topic: Master the past tense of common irregular verbs like "to be," "to have," "to go," "to see," and "to do."

Module 3: Object Pronoun Suffixes
  File: lesson-a2-3.html
  Topic: Learn to use suffixes for "me," "you," "him/her/it," "us," "them" attached to verbs.

Module 4: Past Events & Simple Narratives
  File: lesson-a2-4.html
  Topic: Combine past tenses and sequencing words to tell simple stories and recount past events.

Module 5: Future Tense with `Ghadi`
  File: lesson-a2-5.html
  Topic: Learn to talk about future plans and intentions using `ghadi` and future time expressions.

Module 6: More Prepositions
  File: lesson-a2-6.html
  Topic: Expand your knowledge of prepositions, focusing on time, movement, and purpose.

Module 7: Advanced Adjectives & Descriptions
  File: lesson-a2-7.html
  Topic: Learn more adjectives for personality and appearance, use multiple adjectives, and intensifiers.

Module 8: Comparative & Superlative Adjectives
  File: lesson-a2-8.html
  Topic: Learn how to make comparisons (more than, as...as) and express superlatives (the most).

Module 9: Health & Body
  File: lesson-a2-9.html
  Topic: Learn vocabulary for body parts, common health issues, and doctor/pharmacy interactions.

Module 10: Daily Routines & Hobbies (Extended)
  File: lesson-a2-10.html
  Topic: Describe daily activities in more detail, discuss hobbies, and use adverbs of frequency.

Module 11: Transportation & Travel (Extended)
  File: lesson-a2-11.html
  Topic: Learn vocabulary for transport types, buying tickets, and describing simple journeys.

Module 12: Larger Numbers & Quantities
  File: lesson-a2-12.html
  Topic: Learn numbers beyond 100 (hundreds, thousands), fractions, and more complex quantity expressions.

Module 13: Relative Clauses with `lli`
  File: lesson-a2-13.html
  Topic: Learn to connect ideas and add more information about nouns using `lli` (that/which/who).

Module 14: Simple Conditional "If"
  File: lesson-a2-14.html
  Topic: Learn to express conditions and possible outcomes using `ila` (if) for real future possibilities.

Module 15: Opinions & Suggestions
  File: lesson-a2-15.html
  Topic: Learn how to express your opinions and make suggestions for activities or plans.

Module 16: Review of Verb Tenses
  File: lesson-a2-16.html
  Topic: Consolidate your understanding of present, past, and future tenses and practice using them.

Module 17: Cultural Insights & Common Expressions
  File: lesson-a2-17.html
  Topic: Learn common idiomatic expressions, simple proverbs, and more about cultural etiquette.

Module 18: A2 Consolidation & Moving Forward
  File: lesson-a2-18.html
  Topic: Review all A2 topics, practice with mixed exercises, and prepare for the B1 Intermediate level!


B1 INTERMEDIATE COURSE (18 Lessons)
-----------------------------------

Module 1: A2 Review & Advanced Past Tense
  File: lesson-b1-1.html
  Topic: Consolidate A2 knowledge and delve deeper into past tense, focusing on irregular, 'hollow', and 'weak' verbs, and narrative techniques.

Module 2: The Imperfect (Habitual Past)
  File: lesson-b1-2.html
  Topic: Learn to describe habitual or ongoing actions in the past using the "kan + ka-verb" structure (e.g., "I used to...", "I was -ing").

Module 3: Active & Passive Participles
  File: lesson-b1-3.html
  Topic: Understand and form active (doer) and passive (done-to) participles, and their use as adjectives or in simple passive voice.

Module 4: Expressing Ability, Possibility & Permission
  File: lesson-b1-4.html
  Topic: Learn to use `qder` (can), `yemken` (maybe/it's possible), and phrases for asking/giving permission.

Module 5: Expressing Obligation & Necessity
  File: lesson-b1-5.html
  Topic: Master `khass` (must/need), `Daruri` (necessary), `lazem` (it is necessary) to talk about obligations and give advice.

Module 6: More Complex Conditionals
  File: lesson-b1-6.html
  Topic: Explore present and past unreal conditionals ("If I had..., I would...") and using `lu kan`.

Module 7: Advanced Relative Clauses & `ashmn`
  File: lesson-b1-7.html
  Topic: Deepen your use of `lli` including with prepositions, and learn to use `ashmn` (which).

Module 8: Derived Verb Forms I (Causative)
  File: lesson-b1-8.html
  Topic: Introduction to verb patterns (awzan), focusing on Form II (`fe33el`) for causative and intensive meanings.

Module 9: Derived Verb Forms II (Reflexive/Passive)
  File: lesson-b1-9.html
  Topic: Explore Form V (`tfe33el`) and Form VII (`nfe3el`) for reflexive and passive meanings.

Module 10: More Connectors & Discourse Markers
  File: lesson-b1-10.html
  Topic: Learn advanced connectors for contrast, cause/reason, result, and adding information.

Module 11: Reported Speech (Simple)
  File: lesson-b1-11.html
  Topic: Learn to report what others said (`qal belli...`) and asked (`suwwlat wash...`).

Module 12: Describing in Detail
  File: lesson-b1-12.html
  Topic: Practice using a wide range of adjectives, relative clauses, and comparisons for rich descriptions.

Module 13: Expressing Emotions & Feelings
  File: lesson-b1-13.html
  Topic: Expand vocabulary for emotions and learn structures to express how you feel more precisely.

Module 14: Discussing Social & Cultural Topics
  File: lesson-b1-14.html
  Topic: Gain vocabulary for traditions, holidays, and daily life, and practice expressing opinions on these topics.

Module 15: Handling Problems & Complaints
  File: lesson-b1-15.html
  Topic: Learn phrases for expressing dissatisfaction and asking for help with simple problems.

Module 16: Understanding Different Registers
  File: lesson-b1-16.html
  Topic: Develop an awareness of variations in Darija (formal, informal, influence of French/MSA).

Module 17: Extended Narratives & Storytelling
  File: lesson-b1-17.html
  Topic: Practice telling longer, more detailed stories in the past, using varied vocabulary and connectors.

Module 18: B1 Consolidation & Moving to B2
  File: lesson-b1-18.html
  Topic: Comprehensive review of B1 grammar and vocabulary, and guidance for B2 Upper Intermediate.

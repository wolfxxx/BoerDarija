A2 Module 10: Daily Routines & Hobbies

Learn to describe your daily activities in more detail, discuss hobbies, and use adverbs of frequency.

Review: Present Tense (`ka-`) and Time

Remember from A1, you use the `ka-` prefix for habitual actions (e.g., `kanfiq` - I wake up, `kanakul` - I eat). You also learned basic time expressions like `f s-sba7` (in the morning), `kul nhar` (every day).

We'll build on this to describe a more complete daily routine.

Adverbs of Frequency

These words tell you how often something happens. They usually come before the main verb or at the beginning of the sentence.

Example: هو قليل فين كايخرج. (Huwa qlil fin kaykhruj.) - He rarely goes out.

Talking About Hobbies & Free Time

How to say you like doing certain activities (review from A1 Module 17 `kay3jbni`) and vocabulary for common hobbies.

Structure: `kay3jbni` (it pleases me / I like) + `n-` + Verb Stem

Example: كايعجبني نقرا. (Kay3jbni nqra.) - I like to read.

Example Daily Routine Narrative

كل نهار، كانفيق ف السبعة د الصباح. كانغسل وجهي و كانلبس حوايجي. من بعد، كانفطر. ديما كانشرب قهوة. ف التمنية، كانمشي للخدمة. كانخدم حتى للخمسة د العشية. ملي كانرجع للدار، مرة مرة كانتفرج ف التلفزة و لا كانقرا شي كتاب. ف السبعة، كانتعشى مع عائلتي. و ف العشرة د الليل، كانعس.

Kul nhar, kanfiq f s-sb3a d s-sba7. Kanghsel wejhi u kanlbes 7wayji. Mn ba3d, kanfter. Dima kanshrb qhwa. F t-tmnya, kanmshi l l-khedma. Kankhdem 7tta l l-khmsa d l-3shiya. Melli kanrje3 l d-dar, mrra mrra kantferrej f t-telfaza wella kanqra shi ktab. F s-sb3a, kant3esha m3a 3a'ilti. U f l-3shra d l-lil, kan3es.

Every day, I wake up at 7 in the morning. I wash my face and I get dressed (my clothes). After that, I have breakfast. I always drink coffee. At 8, I go to work. I work until 5 in the evening. When I return home, sometimes I watch TV or I read a book. At 7, I have dinner with my family. And at 10 at night, I sleep.

Vocabulary from Routine:
  • `wejhi` (وجهي) - my face
  • `7wayji` (حوايجي) - my clothes
  • `melli` (ملي) - when (conjunction)
  • `shi ktab` (شي كتاب) - a book / some book
  • `3a'ilti` (عائلتي) - my family

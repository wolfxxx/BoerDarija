A2 Module 2: Past Tense (Common Irregular Verbs & Full Conjugation)

Continue mastering the past tense by learning key irregular verbs and practicing full conjugations.

Irregular Verbs in the Past Tense

While many Darija verbs follow a somewhat regular pattern in the past tense (as seen in A2 Module 1), some of the most common verbs are irregular. This means their stems or endings change in ways that don't perfectly fit the standard pattern. These need to be memorized.

We'll focus on the full conjugation (all pronouns) for these important verbs.

Verb: "to be" - `kan` (كان)

The verb "to be" in the past tense is highly irregular and very important for saying "I was," "you were," etc.

Pronoun Darija (Script) Transcription Meaning Audio
Ana (I)كنتkuntI was
Nta (You m.s.)كنتيkuntiYou were
Nti (You f.s.)كنتيkuntiYou were
Huwa (He)كانkanHe was
Hiya (She)كانتkanetShe was
7na (We)كناkunnaWe were
Ntuma (You pl.)كنتوkuntuYou (pl) were
Huma (They)كانوkanuThey were

Example: البارح كنت ف السوق. (L-bare7 kunt f s-souq.) - Yesterday I was in the market.

Example: هو كان مريض. (Huwa kan mrid.) - He was sick.

Verb: "to have" (in the past) - `kan 3nd-` (كان عند)

To say "I had," "you had," etc., you combine the past tense of "to be" (`kan`) with the `3nd-` + suffix structure.

Pronoun Darija (Script) Transcription Meaning Audio
Anaكان عنديkan 3ndiI had
Nta/Ntiكان عندكkan 3ndekYou had
Huwaكان عندوkan 3nduHe had
Hiyaكان عندهاkan 3ndhaShe had
7naكان عندناkan 3ndnaWe had
Ntumaكان عندكمkan 3ndkumYou (pl) had
Humaكان عندهمkan 3ndhumThey had

Example: العام اللي فات كان عندي طموبيل زوينة. (L-3am lli fat kan 3ndi ttomobil zwina.) - Last year I had a nice car.

Verb: "to go" - `msha` (مشى)

The verb "to go" is irregular in the past tense. Note the stem change for some pronouns.

Pronoun Darija (Script) Transcription Meaning Audio
AnaمشيتmshitI went
Nta/NtiمشيتيmshitiYou went
HuwaمشىmshaHe went
HiyaمشاتmshatShe went
7naمشيناmshinaWe went
NtumaمشيتوmshituYou (pl) went
HumaمشاوmshawThey went

Example: البارح مشينا للسينما. (L-bare7 mshina l s-sinima.) - Yesterday we went to the cinema.

Verb: "to see" - `shaf` (شاف)

The verb "to see" also has some irregularities.

Pronoun Darija (Script) Transcription Meaning Audio
AnaشفتsheftI saw
Nta/NtiشفتيsheftiYou saw
HuwaشافshafHe saw
HiyaشافاتshafetShe saw
7naشفناshefnaWe saw
NtumaشفتوsheftuYou (pl) saw
HumaشافوshafuThey saw

Example: شفت واحد الفيلم زوين البارح. (Sheft wa7ed l-film zwin l-bare7.) - I saw a nice film yesterday.

Memorization & Patterns: While these are "irregular," you'll start to see sub-patterns within irregular verbs too. The key is lots of exposure and practice.

Verb: "to do / to make" - `dar` (دار)

This is a very versatile and common irregular verb.

Pronoun Darija (Script) Transcription Meaning Audio
AnaدرتdertI did/made
Nta/NtiدرتيdertiYou did/made
HuwaدارdarHe did/made
HiyaدارتdaretShe did/made
7naدرناdernaWe did/made
NtumaدرتوdertuYou (pl) did/made
HumaداروdaruThey did/made

Example: شنو درتي البارح؟ (Shnu derti l-bare7?) - What did you (m/f) do yesterday?

Example: درت واحد الكيكة. (Dert wa7ed l-kika.) - I made a cake.

Practice Time!

Exercise 1: Fill in the Past Tense Verb

Use the correct past tense form of the verb in parentheses.

  1. Ana kunt ف مراكش السيمانة اللي فاتت. (`kan` - to be)
  2. Huwa msha للسوق البارح. (`msha` - to go)
  3. Hiya shafet واحد الطموبيل حمرا. (`shaf` - to see)
  4. 7na kan 3ndna بزاف د الشغل. (`kan 3nd-` - to have)
  5. Ntuma dertu تمارين مزيان. (`dar` - to do)

Exercise 2: Translate to Darija

Translate these sentences using the past tense:

  1. She was at home. Kanet f d-dar.
  2. I went to the bank. Mshit l l-banka.
  3. What did he see? Shnu shaf?
  4. We had many books. Kan 3ndna bzzaf d l-ktub.
  5. They made tagine. Daru ttajin.
Irregular verbs are learned through practice and repetition. Try to make your own sentences about what you or others did yesterday or last week!
